we are atherton chess club

Our club serves the needs of chess players in the Atherton, Leigh, Tyldesley and Westhoughton areas, although we have members from even further afield.
We are a friendly club that meets on Monday evenings, from 7:30pm, in a private function room at the Atherton Arms. Players of all standards and abilities (including juniors) are always welcome.
If you would like to visit on a club night please contact Club Chair Martin Lythgoe and we will ensure that there is somebody there to greet you and give you a game.

The club enters teams in the Warrington League, Central Lancashire League and NCCU Club Championships, with varying degrees of success! We are affiliated to the Lancashire Chess Association and many members have played for Lancashire's representative teams in recent years.
